I would not say it was very difficult because it is exactly like the problem, we did in Lab7. Referring to invoice1, we started adding comments to our code. I think that this is something that Professor Kazman is trying to get us to familiarize ourselves with.

Smartphoneproducts2 was about creating a loop to display the various information in our javascript. We used "i" in increments to display the information for example, name1 would become name2 since we used eval() to turn the strings into the value we want. This one was straight-forward because it was almost the same as our in class lab on loops.

I will stick to my method of preparing for WODs, which is reviewing the lab before attempting it for now. I think my formula works for now, but if I had to do something different, I would try to worry less about the amount of time I take to finish the WODs.
